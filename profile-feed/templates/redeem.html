Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn Voyage Coins?

You earn Voyage Coins through various activities including completing voyages, making bookings, and participating in special promotions.

When do rewards expire?

Rewards are valid for 12 months from the date of redemption. Please ensure you have enough coins before redeeming.

Can I transfer rewards to another member?

Yes, you can transfer rewards to other NTS Global Voyage members through your account settings.

How do I redeem rewards?

Simply select the reward you want, click 'Redeem Now', and follow the instructions to complete your redemption.
